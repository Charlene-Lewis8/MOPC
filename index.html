MISSION
Rooted in a legacy of faith since 1930, Mount Olive Pentecostal Church stands as a beacon of hope and renewal in the Smyrna community. Empowered by the Holy Spirit, we are devoted to making disciples through sound Christian teaching, nurturing families through compassionate outreach, and serving our neighbors in love.
With deep roots in our community and eyes toward the future, we are committed to equipping believers through discipleship and education, strengthening families through faith-based outreach, and serving as a lighthouse of compassion and hope for generations to come.
